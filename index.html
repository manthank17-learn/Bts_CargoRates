Import Clearance Information

BTS Clearing & Forwarding Limited provides comprehensive clearance services for various cargo types entering Uganda through multiple ports of entry. Our services include:

  • Documentation Handling: Complete processing of all required customs documentation
  • Duty & Tax Calculation: Expert assessment of applicable duties and taxes
  • Customs Representation: Professional representation with customs authorities
  • Cargo Inspection: Coordination of inspection procedures
  • Warehousing & Storage: Temporary storage facilities when needed
  • Transportation: Inland transportation from port to destination

The calculator provides estimates based on current rates and regulations. For specific inquiries or specialized cargo handling requirements, please contact our customer service team.

Help & Tips

To get the most accurate cost estimate, follow these guidelines:

  1. Cargo Type: Select the appropriate category that best matches your shipment
  2. CIF Value: Enter the total Cost, Insurance, and Freight value in USD
  3. HS Code: If known, enter the Harmonized System code for more accurate duty calculation
  4. Service Level: Choose the service level based on your timeline and requirements
  5. Insurance: Select "Yes" if additional cargo insurance is required

Note: This calculator provides estimates only. Final costs may vary based on actual clearance conditions, additional services required, or changes in regulations.
